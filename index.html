ABOUT US

At GeoGuide, we make geography fun and easy to explore! Our platform helps you study Europe’s unique road signs, climates, and landscapes perfect for leveling up your GeoGuessr skills. We’re also the team behind GeoMaster, a free game where you can test your guessing skills with map navigation, pinning, and scoring no subscription needed. We’re a small but passionate team that loves building tools to help players learn, play, and explore the world. Start your journey with us today!
